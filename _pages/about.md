My general research interest lies in the intersectin of robotics and computer vision. Specifically, I'm interested in multimodality (multiple input modality / multiple mode distribution) and generalization. 

I had the priviledge of working with Professor [Li Yi](https://ericyi.github.io) on hand-object interaction understanding, and Professor [Yongjin Liu](http://cg.cs.tsinghua.edu.cn/people/~Yongjin/Yongjin.htm) on 3D vision at Tsinghua University. I also carried out summer research in [R-Pad Lab](https://r-pad.github.io) in CMU RI, guided by Professor [David Held](http://davheld.github.io) and [Ben Eisner](https://beisner.me).
